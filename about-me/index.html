About me

Hi, my name is Barriella Wertheimer, a web development enthusiast with a background in psychology. I’ve been learning HTML and CSS and have a solid grasp of the fundamentals, while also working on improving my JavaScript and Python skills. I enjoy coding because it allows me to be creative while solving problems in unique ways. Currently, I’m exploring both front-end and UX design, and I’m excited to see how these skills come together in my projects.

In addition to web development, I’ve been working as a certified peer specialist and CASAC-T, helping individuals navigate challenges with substance use. However, I’m shifting my focus toward tech as I’ve found it aligns more with my skills and interests. I’m still exploring where I want to go next in my tech career, but I’m enjoying the process of learning and growing.

Feel free to explore my projects, and if you’d like to connect, don’t hesitate to reach out!"
